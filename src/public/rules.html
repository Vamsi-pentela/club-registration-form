Club Rules & Guidelines

1. Membership Requirements

  • Members must be actively registered in the club.
  • Age or qualification restrictions may apply based on club activities.
  • Members must complete all required forms and pay membership fees on time.

2. Code of Conduct

  • Respect others at all times.
  • Maintain inclusivity without discrimination or harassment.
  • Display professionalism in all club activities.

3. Participation and Attendance

  • Attend a minimum number of meetings or events (e.g., at least 70% per month).
  • Participate actively and volunteer when possible.
  • Arrive on time, and inform the coordinator if you can’t attend.

4. Communication and Confidentiality

  • Use designated club communication channels.
  • Keep club matters confidential, especially personal member information.
  • Provide feedback respectfully and privately to club leaders.

5. Dress Code

  • Dress appropriately for each event.
  • Follow specific dress codes as required.

6. Rules for Using Club Resources

  • Use equipment and resources responsibly.
  • Book resources in advance and adhere to reservations.
  • Clean up and return resources in original condition.

7. Financial Guidelines

  • Pay membership fees on time.
  • Seek approval for fundraising activities.
  • Manage funds transparently.

8. Disciplinary Action

  • Minor infractions may result in warnings; severe violations may lead to suspension or expulsion.
  • Members can appeal disciplinary actions.
  • Zero tolerance for harassment, theft, or violence.

9. Personal Development and Learning

  • Participate in skill-building workshops and training.
  • Senior members may mentor new members.

10. Event and Activity Rules

  • Represent the club positively in all public settings.
  • Register for events on time.
  • Volunteer for event organization, setup, or admin tasks.

11. Feedback and Suggestions

  • Submit feedback through the club’s suggestion box.
